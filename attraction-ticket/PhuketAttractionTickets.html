Phuket Attraction Tickets

Hanuman World Phuket

Delve into adventure at Hanuman World, an exhilarating zipline park where visitors can soar through the jungle, tackle obstacle courses, and enjoy panoramic views of Phuket's lush landscape.

Phuket Fantasea

Immerse yourself in a cultural extravaganza at Phuket Fantasea, a theme park offering a blend of Thai mythology, vibrant performances, and an array of entertainment options suitable for the whole family.

Carnival Magic Phuket

Experience the wonder of Carnival Magic, an amusement park featuring thrilling rides, games, and entertainment suitable for both children and adults, promising a day of fun and excitement.

Koh Phi Phi and Bamboo Island Full Day Trip

Explore the breathtaking beauty of Koh Phi Phi and Bamboo Island on a full-day excursion, discovering pristine beaches, crystal-clear waters, and stunning landscapes.

Phi Phi Island Day Tour by Ferry from Phuket

Embark on a relaxing day tour to Phi Phi Island via ferry, allowing visitors to enjoy the scenic journey and explore the natural beauty and attractions of Phi Phi at their own pace.

Phi Phi Speedboat Tour

Opt for a thrilling speedboat tour to Phi Phi and Khai Islands, offering snorkeling opportunities, picturesque beaches, and a chance to witness the stunning marine life.

James Bond and Khai Island Day Trip by Speedboat from Phuket

Discover the iconic James Bond Island and Khai Islands on a speedboat excursion, enjoying snorkeling, sightseeing, and soaking in the mesmerizing island landscapes.

Phi Phi Island Joined/Private Speedboat Tour from Phuket

Choose between a shared or private speedboat tour to Phi Phi Island, offering flexibility and personalized experiences to explore the islands' beauty.

Amanpuri Phuket

Experience luxury and tranquility at Amanpuri Phuket, a renowned resort offering lavish accommodations, world-class amenities, and stunning views of the Andaman Sea, promising an indulgent stay in Phuket.
